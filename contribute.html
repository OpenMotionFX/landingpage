The Licensing Philosophy

We've seen too many "open" projects lock crucial features behind subscription paywalls or proprietary apps once they get popular. OpenMotionFX is defensively licensed to ensure it stays free forever. 

omfx docs --license-audit

SOFTWARE ENGINE : GPLv3
> Ensures derivative UI/Apps must remain open source.

HARDWARE CAD    : CERN-OHL-S
> You can 3D print, modify, and even sell the rigs.
> But you must share your modified CAD files.

COMM PROTOCOL   : CC0 (Public Domain)
> Anyone can build third-party hardware that
> speaks the .omfx wireless protocol freely.
